Journey, Week 0/26. The grind begins.

Journey Week 0/26

Mar 10, 2017 #Journey

This is the beginning of my 6 month (26 weeks) journey. The goal of this journey is to sell stuff online. I’ve decided to focus on selling wordpress plugins and services, even though I have 0 experience with PHP. I also have 0 experience with wordpress. It’s gonna be fun.

Meanwhile, I’ll be working on my master thesis.

Also, I want to loose some weight. I currently weigh 104 kilos, or 230 pounds. I’ve been lifting weights for about 3 years now, so some of that weight is muscle. But I’m also fat. Which sucks.

Today, March 10th of 2017, was the 1st day of my journey. It was a terrible day for me. Another day wasted on reddit. One may argue that being on reddit is not wasting time, but in my case, I think it is. There were other things I wish I was doing.

I guess it’s not fair for me to say “I wish I wasn’t on reddit”, because something in my head clearly “wished” to be on reddit. A more correct statement would be “a part of me wished I was not on reddit”. This is the part of me that actually cares about me. The part of me that wants me to survive, and to excel, and to live a great life. With this journey, I hope to work on this part of myself, making it stronger, while slowly weakening the opposing, lazy part of myself.

I will be making weekly blog posts updates on my journey. I’m posting this on my blog to keep me on track, while simultaneously hoping that someone, somewhere, will follow along.

It’s on.
